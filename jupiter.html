-JUPITER-

Jupiter in astrology represents growth, luck, and wisdom. It symbolizes opportunities for expansion and abundance in various aspects of life. Jupiter's influence is generally positive, bringing good fortune and a broad perspective. It encourages optimism, generosity, and a pursuit of higher knowledge and spirituality. Its placement in a birth chart provides insights into a person's potential for positive experiences and personal growth.
